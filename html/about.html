Career Opportunities at RMA-Ford Cambodia
A Career at RMA-Ford Cambodia is an opportunity for Life

Our culture is entrepreneurial, fast–paced, challenging, respectful, fun, professional and highly customer-focused. We actively and aggressively look for people who thrive in this kind of environment and share our work ethic and values. We will always recognize and reward contributions consistent with them. People drive our business, its growth and success. We rely on the character, skills, attributes and energy of each person, and will take these factors into account in making employment decisions.
Hard work, dedication to excellence and ultimately the satisfaction of a “job well done” is how the Ford Cambodia culture can be defined.
